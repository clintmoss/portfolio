Holly Springs Laser & Eye

  • Problem: New doctor and new practice, but no website yet
  • Process: Client & Designer Meetings, Design Drafts, Built on Duda
  • Solution: A new website to kick off their ophthalmologist practice

I designed this website for an ophthalmologist. They didn’t have a website, but were about to buy a property, so they needed one fast. Some of the design elements were dictated by the client, but for the most part, I did the designing and branding. The website was simple, SEO-optimized, and built quickly according to the client’s design preferences. The client was really happy with the site and had all the pages they needed starting off.
