Online Textbook

  • Problem: Textbooks are too expensive and there are a lot of issues around coordinating paperback textbooks for online courses
  • Process: Meet with Subject Matter Expert, organize and digitalize textbook, create online version
  • Solution: A accessible, free, and user-friendly textbook for students

I built a textbook for an online math course that incorporates functionality and navigation specifically for users to take in large amounts of text. The physical textbook for this course was not available in an online format. Instead of displaying a huge PDF for students to study, the math department wanted a more interactive and easy-to-use online textbook. Using dark mode, side-collapsing navigation, and design styles, I created a user-friendly online textbook.
