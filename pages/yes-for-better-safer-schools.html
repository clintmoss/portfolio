Yes For Better Safer Schools

  • Problem: Too many schools in Arizona are underfunded. A campaign to push for new propositions needed an online portal for donations and awareness
  • Process: Meeting with clients, create custom accordions, account for dual-language website, built on Wix
  • Solution: A bi-lingual custom Wix website for Arizona users

I built this website to support the Tucson Unified School District in their 2023 bond and override campaign. The client wanted a straightforward site that implemented their existing branding. The completed website is available in Spanish and English and serves as a portal for donations. It features an accordion on the FAQ page built using custom code. The result was a functional, user-friendly, and on-brand website on a tight turnaround.
