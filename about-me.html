About Me


I'm Clint a web developer and designer.

I've worked with a lot of different companies and built many different websites. I started at BYU doing some CS classes, let school go to get married, get a career, and do a coding bootcamp. I've been mostly self-taught but am currently finishing my degree at ASU in their internet and web development major.

I have the best little family of four. I say that because my family has 12 kids in it and my wife is the oldest of 8. We love traveling, eating food, cooking mean Hawaiian BBQ, and working together. My wife is currently in school and working as we raise our family. She also owns her own graphic design and branding company and I've been subcontracting and working for other companies helping them with their websites.
